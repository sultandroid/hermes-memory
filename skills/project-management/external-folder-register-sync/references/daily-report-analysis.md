## Gap Detection

When scanning daily reports, always check if June and July folders exist and contain files. If they exist but are empty, flag this as a reporting gap. The May 2026 reports were consistent (daily except weekends), but June and July 2026 had zero reports filed as of late July — a 54+ day gap.
