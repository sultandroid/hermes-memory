# Client-Facing Document Content Rules — Aseer Museum

## Core Principle

As the contractor (Samaya), all project documents must read as factual, professional, and neutral — never self-incriminating, never promotional, never naming unapproved parties.

## Rules

### 1. No Brand Names in Materials Sections

Materials sections (e.g., Section 3.0 of Resource Plan) must use **generic descriptions only**:

- ❌ `Porcelain & mosaic tiles (Ceramiche Piemme), carpet (Tarkett), acoustic panels (Kvadrat)`
- ✅ `Porcelain & mosaic tiles, carpet, acoustic panels`

Exception: Specialist subcontractors listed in Tier 2/Appendix B can use their firm name for scope identification (e.g., "ZNA Studio — Lighting Design").

### 2. No Self-Incriminating Language

Never write anything that could be used to claim the contractor is failing an obligation:

- ❌ `TBC — ⚠ CRITICAL PATH` (suggests we are holding up the project)
- ✅ `TBC` (factual — no judgment)

Avoid: "CRITICAL PATH", "delayed", "behind schedule", "at risk", "failure", "deficiency", "problem" in reference to Samaya's own performance. Use neutral terms like "pending", "in progress", "to be confirmed", "awaiting".

### 3. Don't Name Unapproved Consultants/Persons

If a consultant or person has not been formally approved by MoC/CG:

- ❌ `Dr. Ehab Foda — LEED / Mostadam` (exposes unapproved name)
- ❌ `Lumotion` (firm not yet approved)
- ✅ `Sustainability Strategy — per ER embedded requirement`
- ✅ `Interactive Applications — prequalification in progress`

Safety rule: unless a name/firm appears in an approved KPR or approved submittal, use `—` or a generic description.

### 4. Sustainability Specialist Per ER

Even if not listed in Appendix B, the Sustainability Specialist role must appear because the ER requires it. Cite as `per ER embedded requirement` not `per Appendix B`.

### 5. Role Corrections (from user)

- Mohamed Mostafa = **Technical Office Mechanical Engineer**, not BIM modeler
- Electrical Engineer = on site, not just HO — use `Electrical Engineer` without (HO) suffix
- Junior engineer names should not appear in the plan

### 6. Generalized Materials List Format

Use this format for materials sections:

```
<b>Flooring</b> — Porcelain & mosaic tiles, micro concrete effect tiles, marble & limestone, stone setts, carpet
<b>Walls & Ceilings</b> — Paint, acoustic plasterboard, metal baffle ceiling, fibre-reinforced gypsum, acoustic panels
```
