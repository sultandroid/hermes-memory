## Prequal Assessment — Common Scope Pitfalls

- **Oddy test**: required only for materials inside showcases or near exhibition objects. Acoustic ceiling panels, baffles, wall fabric, and floor underlay do not need Oddy — they don't contact exhibits. Do not over-specify.
- **BIM coordination**: RCP federation, clash detection, and model integration are Samaya's responsibility, not the subcontractor's. Subcontractors provide layout drawings, equipment data, and MEP input specs — not BIM coordination.
- **ISO certs**: many KSA sub-consultants of SDE's size do not hold ISO 9001/14001/45001. SCE Consultant classification + project track record may be accepted as equivalent by CG.
