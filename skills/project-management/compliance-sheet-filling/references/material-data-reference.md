## MAR Review PDF Structure

The Engineer's SAF-0280-Q-MAR-AR-1112-00 review PDF (310 pages, 80 MB) contains:
- Pages 1-2: MAR form with Engineer's Code C comments
- Pages 3-6: Comment Resolution Sheet + MAR Checklist
- Pages 7-250: All datasheets, catalogs, test reports
- Pages 251-262: Old compliance sheets (mostly "Noted" — rejected by Engineer)
- Pages 279-298: Legal certificates section (ISO, CR, VAT, GOSI, Saudization)
- Pages 298-304: NHC project completion certificates
- Page 310: SASO compliance statement
