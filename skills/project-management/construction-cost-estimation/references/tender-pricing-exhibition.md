## Critical Observations from This Project

1. **The AV BOQ (RCRC BOQ 15.12.25 rev0.xlsx) has blank unit prices** — quantities and technical specs are complete but EUR rates are empty. This is common: the designer provides the spec sheet, the contractor fills pricing via AV subcontractor quotes.

2. **Lighting has dual currencies** — the designer's raw file (Andrea251123 sheet) is in EUR, the summary sheet (con dolares 271125) applies a EUR-to-USD conversion rate of 1 EUR = 1.1585 USD. Verify this is current.

3. **Add-on percentages are fixed** — the BoQ applies them sequentially (3% art direction, 5% mobilisation, 7% overhead, 2% financial cost, 5% contingency) but doesn't specify whether they compound or are applied to the base. Verify this with procurement.

4. **AV hardware plus installation** — the EX AV HARDWARE sheet separates equipment ($1.69M) from installation & commissioning ($654K) under 'AV Control & Installation' ($744K total). The installation line is bundled with AV control equipment; verify the breakdown.
