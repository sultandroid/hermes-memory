# NRS Negotiation Analysis — 2026-06-06 Worked Example

## The Situation

Nissen Richards Studio (NRS), design subcontractor for Aseer Regional Museum, is paid SAR 812,100 (67%) against EV of only SAR 483K (40% completion). Overpayment = -SAR 329K (CPI=0.59).

NRS proposed: extend the completion date, then split remaining SAR 396,900 into equal monthly instalments.

## Contract Terms Found

**Art. 11 — Payment:**
```
Advance (10%)                  = SAR 120,900
Stage 4 — Monthly Instalments  = Balance of SAR 691,200
Stage 5 — Monthly Instalments  = SAR 270,000
Stages 5-6 — Monthly Instalments = SAR 171,000
```

NRS is technically correct: the contract says "Monthly Instalments" for all stages.

## Counter-Arguments Available

| Contract clause | Argument |
|---|---|
| Art. 11.2: invoicing schedule agreed in advance | Stage 5 invoicing schedule was never formally agreed |
| Art. 5.1: Stage 5 = off-site fab review against IFC | No IFC = no Stage 5 work = paying for work that can't happen |
| Art. 18: written form for amendments | Any revised payment plan must be a formal contract amendment |
| Art. 4.7: IFC delivery in 8 weeks | NRS hasn't delivered IFC (blocked by SI007, but obligation exists) |

## Recommended Position

| Component | Amount | Condition |
|---|---|---|
| Time extension | Granted | Formal amendment |
| Monthly retainer | SAR 20-30K | Deductible from future milestones, not additive |
| IFC delivery package | SAR 100K | Upon approved IFC for 2-3 key packages |
| Specialist packages | SAR 200K (8 x SAR 25K) | Per approved specialist IFC package |
| Final retention | SAR 96,900 | Stage 5-6 sign-off |
| **Total remaining** | **~SAR 397K** | |

## What to Say to NRS

> "We agree the time extension is warranted. But you've been paid 67% to deliver 40% of the work — SAR 329K more than earned value. We cannot pay the remaining SAR 397K on elapsed time. We propose a package-by-package release tied to specialist IFC approvals."

## Red Lines

- Do NOT accept equal monthly instalments
- Keep INV-4825 (SAR 90K) on HOLD
- Don't release Stage 5 money before IFC is substantially done
- Document everything via a formal contract amendment (Art. 18)
