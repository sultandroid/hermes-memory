## Key Gaps

1. **No museum/cultural facility in portfolio** — all residential/commercial/streetscape
2. **Scale mismatch** — largest projects are private villas and a mall. No multi-disciplinary coordination (structural, MEP drainage, lighting, graphics)
3. **High-altitude horticulture** — Aseer (Abha ~2,200m) has unique microclimate. Evergreen's portfolio is entirely hot lowland. No evidence of Aseer-region nursery relationships
4. **Team depth** — 1 Landscape Architect + 2 Engineers is thin for design + shop drawings + installation supervision + 90-day establishment period

## Verdict

**Not recommended for sole appointment.** Evergreen is a competent residential/commercial landscaping contractor but lacks the museum/cultural portfolio and high-altitude experience.

**Recommendation:**
- Keep on file as potential **execution subcontractor** (planting/irrigation installation under a design lead)
- Design lead should be a firm with proven museum/cultural landscape portfolio
- If no better candidate emerges, Evergreen would need to partner with a design consultant who has the missing experience
