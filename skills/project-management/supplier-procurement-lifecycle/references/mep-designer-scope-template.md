# MEP Designer SCOPE_REQUEST Template

When building a SCOPE_REQUEST for an MEP Designer subcontractor (construction fit-out/museum), use this 12-section structure. Adjust sections per project.

## Standard Structure

| # | Section | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Project Overview | Contract ref, project description, design standards |
| 2 | Scope of Services | What the MEP Designer actually delivers |
| 3 | Mechanical Systems | HVAC, BMS, controls, gallery environment |
| 4 | Electrical Systems | Power, small power, lighting, SEC coordination |
| 5 | ELV / Low Current Systems | CCTV, access control, MATV, intercom, structured cabling, CITC registration |
| 6 | Fire Protection | Sprinklers, clean agent, kitchen hood, alarm/PA/VA |
| 7 | Plumbing & Drainage | Water supply, drainage, gas |
| 8 | Earthing & Lightning Protection | Earthing system, lightning risk assessment |
| 9 | Telecom / ICT | Structured cabling, FTTH, STC guidelines, wireless |
| 10 | Existing Services Survey | Condition/capacity survey, record drawings |
| 11 | Gallery Environmental & Acoustic | RH stability, acoustic isolation, air quality, conservation-grade conditioning |
| 12 | External Works | External MEP services coordination |

## Key Exclusions to Define Explicitly

| Item | Who Handles (Typical) | Note |
|------|----------------------|------|
| BIM / Revit modeling | Contractor's BIM Unit | Designer provides 2D CAD; Contractor converts to BIM |
| Construction supervision | Separate contract | Design-only engagement |
| AV / IT / Interactive systems | Specialist AV subconsultant | MEP coordinates on routing/sizing only |
| Scenography / Showcase MEP | Specialist subconsultant | MEP provides rough-in points only |
| Fire Life Safety (FLS) Strategy | FLS Consultant | MEP implements FLS requirements |

## Programme / Gate Structure

When the schedule uses Direct-to-IFC (DtIFC) with 5-working-day reviews:

| Phase | Duration | Output |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Mobilization + survey | 10 days | Existing services survey report |
| 50% Design | 14 days | Concept layouts, system selection, sizing |
| CG review | 5 working days | CG conformance review |
| 90% Design | 14 days | Detailed design, schedules, coordination |
| CG review | 5 working days | CG conformance review |
| 100% IFC | 10 days | Construction-ready documents |
| CG review | 5 working days | Final conformance review |
| Closure | 3 days | CRS resolution |

## Documents to Reference in SCOPE_REQUEST

- Employer's Requirements (ER) — basis of design
- SoW — scope boundaries, exclusions
- Project's DMP / SMP — gate structure, review periods
- Consultant's Communication Plan — review SLA
- NRS design drawings (50% or Stage 3) — baseline for the MEP designer to complete
- Existing building as-builts (if available)
