# Worked Example: Aseer Museum Sustainability Proposals (2 June 2026)

## Context
Evaluated two consultancy proposals for sustainability management services on the Aseer Regional Museum of Art project (3092). User initially requested certification-focused evaluation, then corrected: **"التصنيف غير مطلوب — قارن فهم المطلوب، نطاق العمل بالتفصيل"**.

## Proposals
| # | File | Source |
|---|------|--------|
| 1 | CC-ASEER-SUS-PRO-001 Rev 0 | Sustainability Consultancy (unnamed), 17 pages |
| 2 | SG Group Quotation | SG Group for Services, 1-page + terms |

## Key Evaluation Criteria (Post-Correction)
- Understanding of museum-specific challenges (humidity, heritage, Oddy)
- Coverage across all project phases (design → fabrication → construction → handover)
- Field presence / site visit commitment
- Methodology (management vs advisory-only)
- Pricing model (lump-sum vs monthly)

## Contradictions Found in Proposal 1 (Critical Review)
| # | Issue | Severity |
|---|-------|----------|
| 1 | Lump-sum vs time-based in same paragraph | 🔴 High |
| 2 | VAT: "All prices include VAT" vs "All amounts exclude VAT" in same table | 🔴 High |
| 3 | Total SAR: "16,3013" instead of "163,013" (missing digit) | 🔴 High |
| 4 | Unrealistic hours: 630 hrs for 2 experts over ~12 months = 26 hrs/month/expert | 🔴 High |
| 5 | Company unnamed: "Sustainability Consultancy" with no legal entity | 🔴 High |
| 6 | Experts placeholder: "Expert 1" and "Expert 2" with no CVs | 🔴 High |
| 7 | VAT invalid: consultancy is Cairo-based individuals — cannot charge KSA VAT of 15% | 🔴 High |
| 8 | Oddy managed (page 3) but lab costs excluded (page 15) | 🟡 Medium |
| 9 | ITCA coordination claimed but ITCA appointment separate | 🟡 Medium |
| 10 | Site visits mandatory (page 14) but negotiable (page 15) | 🟡 Medium |

## User Corrections Applied
1. **"التصنيف غير مطلوب"** — Removed Mostadam certification from evaluation
2. **"السفر على حساب سامية"** — Travel cost adds ~$10K to total, adjusted recommendation
3. **"الـ VAT وهمي"** — Flagged VAT as invalid for non-KSA entity
4. **"online فقط"** — Removed site visit requirement from expectations

## Outcome
- **Proposal 1: 8.6/10 — Recommended** after corrections and removing VAT
- **SG Group: 4/10 — Not recommended** (generic, advisory-only, no Oddy, zero site visits, monthly pricing risk)
