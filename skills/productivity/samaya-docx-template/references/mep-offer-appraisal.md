## Fee Assessment

Fee: 171,500 SAR for design review/endorsement only (verifier role, not producer).
Scope match: ~30%

## Verdict

Not recommended as lead MEP designer — their offer covers verification/review only, not production. Suitable only as a peer-review consultant if appointed separately from the production designer, but that would duplicate cost and lengthen the schedule. The scope gaps (survey, BIM, ELV, gallery env, commissioning, fast-track) are fundamental and cannot be bridged without a substantially revised offer equating to a full design-fee proposal.
