# CG Support Letter Pattern

Use when generating a short support letter from Samaya to CG recommending a subcontractor for prequalification. This is distinct from the prequal support document (which is for the supplier to fill out and stamp).

## When to use

- CG asks for a prequalification recommendation
- Samaya needs to formally submit a subcontractor for CG approval
- The deliverable is a one-page letter, not a full prequal package

## Structure

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Header | Samaya Investment, Technical Office - BIM Unit, project name, doc ref |
| Title | "PREQUALIFICATION SUPPORT LETTER" |
| To | Consultant Group (CG) |
| Subject | "Prequalification of [Company Name] - [Trade]" |
| Body 1 | Samaya submits [Company] for prequalification as [Trade] for the project |
| Body 2 | Company credentials: CR number, years of experience, relevant portfolio |
| Body 3 | Confirms they reviewed scope documents and understand the work |
| Body 4 | Proposed team summary |
| Body 5 | Samaya recommends approval |
| Attachments | List of attached documents (profile, certs, portfolio) |
| Sign-off | Name, title, company |

## Rules

- One page only. No tables, no forms, no submission requirements for the supplier to fill out.
- No symbols: use "Section" not "§", "-" not "—" or "•", "cafe" not "cafe with accent".
- No AI language: no "seamlessly", "cutting-edge", "state-of-the-art", "bespoke", "delighted to".
- No meta-commentary: no "this document outlines", "the following sections will cover".
- Write as a senior engineer dictating a letter. Short sentences, active voice.
- Attachments list at the bottom as plain bullet list with hanging indent.
- Sign-off block: name, title, company. No signature/stamp block (that's for the supplier doc).
- Doc ref follows project pattern: MOC-ASEER-SIC-1K0-SC-XXX-PREQ-001.

## Example body text

"Samaya Investment submits [Company] for prequalification as the [Trade] for the Aseer Regional Museum project.

[Company] is a Saudi registered company (CR XXXXXXXX) with [X] years of experience in [field] across the Kingdom. Their portfolio includes projects for [clients].

They have reviewed the project scope documents including the Scope of Work (Section X.X), Employer's Requirements, and relevant design drawings. [Company] confirms they understand the full scope of work.

Their proposed team includes [roles]. They hold valid Saudi Contractors Authority membership, ISO certifications, and VAT registration.

Samaya recommends [Company] for approval as a qualified [trade] subcontractor for this project. Their full company profile and supporting documents are attached for your review."

## Distinction from Prequal Support Document

| Document | Audience | Purpose | Length |
|----------|----------|---------|--------|
| CG Support Letter | CG | Samaya recommends supplier for approval | 1 page |
| Prequal Support Document | Supplier | Supplier fills out and stamps to submit to Samaya | Multi-page with forms |
