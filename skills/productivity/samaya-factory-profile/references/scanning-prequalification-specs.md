# 3D Scanning Prequalification — Technical Specifications

Exact parameters for prequalification pages. No brand/device names.

## Core Specs
- **Technology**: Structured blue light 3D scanning
- **Light Source**: Blue LED, no laser
- **Capture Type**: Geometry and texture
- **3D Resolution**: Up to 0.05 mm
- **3D Point Accuracy**: Up to 0.05 mm
- **Accuracy Over Distance**: Up to 0.03% over 100 cm
- **Probing Error**: 0.050 mm
- **Sphere Spacing Error**: 0.050 mm + L × 0.3 mm/m
- **Calibration**: VDI/VDE 2634 Blatt 3:2008-12
- **Pre-heating Time**: Minimum 30 minutes

## Working Conditions
- **Working Distance**: 0.19 – 0.30 m
- **FOV (closest)**: 90 × 70 mm
- **FOV (furthest)**: 180 × 140 mm
- **Angular FOV**: 30° × 21°
- **Frame Rate**: Up to 15 frames/s
- **Exposure Time**: 0.0005 sec
- **Operating Temp**: 5°C – 35°C
- **Markers**: Not required
- **Surface Capability**: Dark, shiny, complex surfaces
- **Ambient Light**: Avoid strong direct sunlight

## Process Flow (8 steps)
Object Review → Surface Check → Scan Setup → Multi-Angle Capture → Data Alignment → Mesh Clean → Dimensional Verification → Model Export

## Application Outputs
Artifact Documentation, Digital Archive, Replica Production, Scale Model Reference, Tactile Model Support, Reverse Engineering, Dimensional Verification, Fabrication Reference
