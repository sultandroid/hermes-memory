# Samaya Proposal — Standard Material Specifications

## Cement Board 12mm (Fiber Cement)
- **Thickness:** 12mm ±0.5mm (BS EN 12467)
- **Density:** ≥ 1.2 g/cm³
- **Flexural strength:** ≥ 12 MPa (longitudinal), ≥ 8 MPa (transverse)
- **Fire rating:** A1 non-combustible (ASTM E136 / BS 476)
- **Water absorption:** ≤ 28% by weight
- **Weight:** ~9.4 kg/m² (~28 kg/board at 1220×2440mm)
- **Standard size:** 1220 × 2440 mm (4′ × 8′)
- **Fixing:** Self-tapping screws at ≤ 400mm centres
- **Joint treatment:** Weather-resistant sealant + aluminium/PVC cover strip
- **Service life:** ≥ 10 years outdoor with periodic maintenance
- **Surface:** Ready for digital print / vinyl wrap / direct paint
- **Application:** Rigid substrate for PVC Flex banner mounting on steel frame

## Jotun Epoxy Mastic (Penguard)
- **Type:** Two-component polyamine-cured epoxy mastic
- **Product codes:** 24240 / 24340
- **DFT:** ≥ 200 µm (can be used as primer/mid-coat/finish/single-coat)
- **Application:** Airless spray (recommended), nozzle 19-25 thou, ≥ 150 bar
- **Thinner:** Jotun Thinner No. 17
- **Pot life:** 1 hour @ 23°C
- **Environment:** Atmospheric and immersed — suitable for Makkah climate
- **Standards:** Can be applied over SSPC-SP2/SP3 (hand/power tool cleaned)
- **NOT galvanized — never mention HDG in proposals**

## PVC Flex Banner (Obeflex Frontlit Premium)
- **Product:** Obeflex Frontlit Premium
- **Weight:** 550 g/sqm
- **Thickness:** 0.52 mm
- **Tensile:** 2600/2800 N/5cm (DIN 53354)
- **Tear resistance:** 320/200 N (DIN 53356)
- **Durability:** 2-3 years outdoor vertical exposure
- **Printing:** Solvent/Eco-solvent/UV — 1440 dpi minimum
- **Reference:** Obekan.pdf in project folder

## LED Flood Light
- **Power:** 300W
- **Protection:** IP66
- **Colour temp:** 5000K (Cool White)
- **Efficacy:** ≥ 120 lm/W
- **CRI:** ≥ 80
- **Beam angle:** 120° (wide) or 90°
- **Lifespan:** ≥ 50,000 hours
- **Spacing:** ~12m between fixtures
- **Mounting:** Brackets on top of steel columns or ground-mounted aimed upward
- **Adjustment:** ±90° tilt angle
