## Project Context

- **Procurement:** Design & Build (D&B) Single Stage — Contractor's Proposals
- **Existing building:** Built 2015, in Abha, Aseer region. Three levels + First Floor
- **Scope:** Museum fit-out, MEPF upgrade, exhibition installation
- **Employer:** Ministry of Culture (MoC) via PMC (ACE Moharram Bakhoum)
- **Consultant:** Consultancy Group (CG)
- **Design Lead:** NRS (Nissen Richards Studio) — Architect of Record
- **Main Contractor:** Samaya Investment

## Handover Requirements — ER §2.7 (17 Items)

All 17 are Samaya's responsibility. Must be checked against each in the deliverable tree:

1. Room Data Sheet compliance
2. Snag list / defects list completion
3. As-Built drawings and documentation
4. Permits and No Objection Certificates (NOCs) from all authorities
5. Operation & Maintenance (O&M) Manuals
6. Warranties and guarantees
7. Spares and consumables schedule + attic stock
8. Keys and access devices
9. ITCA test results and certificates
10. Pest control certificate
11. Building Manual (complete)
12. Health and Safety File
13. Training completion certificates
14. Asset Information (equipment schedules, serials, warranties)
15. Final cleaning certificate
16. Commissioning certificates (MEP, fire, AV, lifts)
17. Practical Completion Certificate

- `[NRS/DESIGN]` — NRS design deliverable managed by Samaya
- `[SAMAYA]` — Direct Samaya responsibility
- `[ER §X.X]` — Reference to specific ER clause
- `[SOW §X.X]` — Reference to specific SOW clause
