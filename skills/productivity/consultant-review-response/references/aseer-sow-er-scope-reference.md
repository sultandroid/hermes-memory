## Review Period Conflict — 14 Calendar vs 14 Working Days

**This is a known textual conflict between ER §2.4.A / SoW §6.7 and SoW §6.5.**

| Clause | Text | Days | Scope |
|--------|------|:----:|-------|
| ER §2.4.A | "The review period, where applicable, shall be 14 calendar days, unless otherwise specified." | 14 **calendar** | General conformance reviews |
| SoW §6.7 | "The review period, where applicable, shall be 14 calendar days, unless otherwise specified." | 14 **calendar** | General approval procedures |
| SoW §6.5 | "The Contractor is to allow for a minimum of 14 working days for stage-end reviews by the MoC and/or PMC." | 14 **working** | Stage-end reviews (50%/90%/100% gates) |

**Resolution framework for Direct-to-IFC approach:**

The Direct-to-IFC mechanism under ER §2.4 eliminates the need for stage-end reviews for listed elements. Therefore:
- Direct-to-IFC elements → 14 **calendar** days per ER §2.4.A
- Staged-route elements (exhibition packages retaining 50%/90% gates) → 14 **working** days per SoW §6.5 for stage-end reviews

**Arguments if CG challenges the calendar-day position:**
1. ER §2.4.A is the controlling clause for the general review regime. SoW §6.5 is a specific sub-clause for stage-end reviews only.
2. For a combined single design phase (per ER §2.4), there are no "stages" for Direct-to-IFC elements — hence §6.5 is not triggered.
3. No subsequent approved document (DMP, BEP, Comm Plan, MOMs) has varied or resolved this conflict.

**Protective language for Schedule Basis document:**

> Per ER §2.4, the contract provides for a single combined design phase. SoW §6.5 (14 working days) applies to stage-end reviews of the staged design route only. For elements proceeding Direct-to-IFC under ER §2.4, the general review period of 14 calendar days per ER §2.4.A and SoW §6.7 applies. No subsequent approved document has varied this.

## Programme Audit — Phase Identification Checklist

When reviewing a schedule, determine its scope first:

| Signal | Design-Phase Only | Full Project |
|--------|------------------|-------------|
| File name contains | "DESIGN PHASE", "DESIGN", "Stage 4" | "MASTER PROGRAMME", "SH-006", "PROJECT" |
| Activity prefix | PE (Prelim), AS (Assessment), EN (Eng/Design), PR (Procurement) | Includes CN (Construction), IN (Install), TC (T&C), HD (Handover) |
| End date | Aligns with IFC/design gate | Aligns with contract completion |
| Parts covered | Part 1, 2, 4 primarily | Parts 1-7 |
| Missing | Off-site Fab, On-site Install, T&C, Handover, Defects | — |
