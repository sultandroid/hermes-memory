## SOW — Designer Definition (Section 1.4, Page 11)

> "Designer means the Contractor or the entity sub-contracted by the Contractor who is responsible for design and documentation of the Works. The Designer can either be an externally appointed design consultancy and/or an inhouse design and engineering team having appropriate and approved qualifications, experience and Authority registration in the disciplines relevant to the Works."

**Key phrase:** "can either be an externally appointed design consultancy **and/or** an inhouse design and engineering team" — establishes two parallel paths.

## SOW — Sub-Consultant Definition (Section 1.4, Page 11)

> "Sub-Consultant means the specialist discipline Contractors or Consultants appointed by the Contractor to undertake parts of their scope of works. Appointment by the Contractor of any Sub-Consultant(s) shall be subject to the Client's approval. Once appointed the Sub-Consultant(s) shall be the full responsibility of the Contractor."

**Key phrase:** "specialist discipline Contractors or Consultants" — MEP design firm qualifies as sub-consultant.

## SOW — Design Team Profile (Section 5.4, Page 19)

> "The profile and proven experience of the Contractor's Design Team are crucial for delivering the design phase of the Exhibition Works. The Contractor must submit profiles of the proposed design team members for approval by the MoC."

> "The Contractor is required to integrate, across its design development and deliverables, specialist advice from reputable industry consultants and specialists, as required."

## SOW — Staffing and Deployments (Section 5.5, Page 20)

> "The Contractor's Personnel shall be appropriately qualified, skilled and experienced in their respective trades or occupations."

> "The Contractor shall ensure that the Key Personnel execute the Contract Works and perform the roles required of their nominated positions and are not removed from those positions without the prior written approval of MoC."

> "Design team/personnel/representative shall be dedicated to the Project and available for design meetings (face to face)."

> Required specialists list includes: **MEP Specialist**, Project Director, AV Hardware Specialist, Setworks/Joinery Specialist, Lighting Specialist, Showcase Specialist, Design & BIM Manager, Structural Engineer, and others.

## SOW — Engineering Design (Section 7.3, Page 38)

> "All design shall be undertaken by suitably qualified, experienced and local registered / licensed engineers."

## ER — Design and Documentation Certification (Section 2.4, Page 14-15)

> "All design must be carried out by suitably qualified, experienced and registered engineers, architects and/or design professionals. Prior to any submittal being made to the PMC for conformance review, all design and documentation must have been:
> a. Checked by a suitably qualified and experienced design professional
> b. Reviewed for quality assurance purposes, in accordance with the Contractor's Project Quality Plan (PQP)
> c. Approved for issued-for-construction (IFC) by the Contractor, or by the Contractor's Designer"

> Certification wording required:
> "We hereby certify that the submitted documents, and design contained therein, have been reviewed and approved by a suitably qualified, experienced and registered engineer, architect and/or design professional."

## Main Contract — Subcontracting (Article 13)

> "The Contractor may subcontract the Works and purchases for a value up to 30% thirty percent of the Contract value, subject to the consent of the Government Entity."

> "The Contractor may also subcontract Works and purchases exceeding 30% of the Contract value and less than 50% fifty percent thereof, provided that it obtains the prior consent of the Spending Efficiency Achievement Center and the Government Entity."

> "The Contractor may not add any new subcontractors without the prior consent of the Government Entity."
