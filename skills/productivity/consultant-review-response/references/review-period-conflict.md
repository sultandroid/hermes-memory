# Review Period Conflict — Calendar vs Working Days

## The Conflict

| Source | Clause | Duration | Status |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| **ER §2.4.A** | *"The review period, where applicable, shall be 14 calendar days, unless otherwise specified."* | 14 **calendar** days | Contractual — Authoritative |
| **SoW §6.7** | *"The review period, where applicable, shall be 14 calendar days, unless otherwise specified."* | 14 **calendar** days | Contractual — Same language as ER |
| **SoW §6.5** | *"The Contractor is to allow for a minimum of 14 working days for stage-end reviews by the MoC and/or PMC."* | 14 **working** days | Contractual — Scoped to stage-end reviews only |

## Resolution Logic

**Applies only to stage-end reviews (50%/90% gate reviews) per SoW §6.5:**
= 14 working days (≈16-18 calendar days in KSA with Friday off)

**Applies to all other conformance reviews (including Direct-to-IFC single submissions):**
= 14 calendar days per ER §2.4.A + SoW §6.7

**For Direct-to-IFC elements:** The staged route (50% → 90% → 100%) is bypassed entirely. There are no "stage-end reviews" for these elements — they proceed straight to IFC conformance review. SoW §6.5 does not apply. ER §2.4.A controls at 14 calendar days.

CG does not consistently adhere to either standard. The SH-006 review (26 calendar days) is the worst case and should be cited if CG challenges the 14-day assumption.

## Recommended Schedule Basis Wording

*"Per ER §2.4.A and SoW §6.7, the conformance review period is 14 calendar days for all submissions. SoW §6.5 (14 working days) applies only to stage-end reviews of the staged design route. For elements proceeding Direct-to-IFC under ER §2.4, the 14-calendar-day period applies. No subsequently approved project document has varied this."*

## If CG Challenges

CG may cite SoW §6.5 (14 working days). Rebuttal:

1. SoW §6.5 explicitly scopes itself to *"stage-end reviews"* — the 50%/90% gates
2. Direct-to-IFC elements have no stage-end reviews — they proceed straight to IFC
3. ER §2.4.A and SoW §6.7 both use *"14 calendar days"* as the general standard
4. ER §2.4 controls over SoW per the precedence clause in the Contract (typically §2 of the Contract)
