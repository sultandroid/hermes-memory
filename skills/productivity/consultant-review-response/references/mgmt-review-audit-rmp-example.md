## Key Distinction

Items 1 & 2 are **appointment actions**, not plan document gaps. The plan correctly defines the roles and flags them as vacant. PD/HR must fill them. Items 3-10 are genuine plan gaps the team must resolve.

## SoW §5.4 Reference for Mandatory Specialist Roles

The PD's items 3, 4, 5 are not arbitrary — they originate from SoW §5.4:

> *"The Contractor shall provide an accessibility consultant to advise and review all exhibit components"*
>
> *"It is expected that the Contractor consults, as required, an acoustics and humidity specialist to update and modify the design as needed to meet the target Acoustic and Humidity performance requirements"*

These are contractually required roles, not optional additions. The plan must reflect them.
