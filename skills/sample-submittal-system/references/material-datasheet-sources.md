# Material Datasheet Sources

Research results from 2026-07-06 search for AISI 304 SS and PVD coating datasheets.

## AISI 304 Stainless Steel Datasheets

### Available (open access)
| Source | URL | Notes |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Wikipedia | `https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/pdf/SAE_304_stainless_steel` | Full article with composition, mechanical properties, applications. 1.1 MB PDF. |
| AZoM Article | `https://www.azom.com/article.aspx?ArticleID=2867` | Comprehensive article with tables. PDF download requires form submission. |

### Blocked / 404 (do not rely on)
- Aalco, Outokumpu, ThyssenKrupp, Penn Stainless, Sandmeyer, AK Steel, Atlas Steels, Metal Supermarkets — all either 403, 404, or require form submission
- GitHub raw content repos — no public repos found with SS304 datasheet PDFs

## Recommendation

For formal submittals, obtain official datasheets from the actual material supplier (Rawasin for PVD, or the stainless steel vendor). The Wikipedia PDFs can serve as interim reference but are not manufacturer-certified datasheets.
