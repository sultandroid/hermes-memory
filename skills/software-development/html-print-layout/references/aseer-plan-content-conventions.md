# Aseer Museum Plan Content Conventions

Content rules for Aseer Museum management plans when producing HTML deliverables for CG review or internal use. These supplement the layout/style rules in the main SKILL.md.

## Document References

- All doc numbers must be verified against the **Document Submittals log** (Register Log). Do NOT guess or infer.
- Do NOT add placeholder doc numbers on the cover (e.g. `REF: MOC-...-PL-00XX`). Leave blank until Document Controller assigns a number.
- Cross-reference every doc ref in Section 1.3 (Authority Basis) and the reference list against the official Register.

## Consultant Names

- **Never name unapproved consultants.** If a firm is not yet approved (prequalification in progress, NDA signed only, submission pending), do NOT list their name. Use `—` (TBC) instead.
- Specifically: Dr. Ehab Foda (sustainability), Lumotion (interactives) — both not yet approved as of 2026-06-24.
- Sustainability entry: refer as "Sustainability Strategy" with scope "per ER embedded requirement". Do NOT reference LEED program or name the consultant.
- For approved firms, use factual status (e.g. "Code B approved 15-Jun", "Approved 11-Feb").

## Materials & Brand Names

- **No brand names** in the Materials section (Section 3.0). Use generic material descriptions only:
  - ✅ "Porcelain & mosaic tiles, micro concrete effect tiles, marble & limestone"
  - ❌ "Ceramiche Piemme, Concept Tiles, Domus, Tarkett"
  - ✅ "Acoustic plasterboard, metal baffle ceiling, fibre-reinforced gypsum"
  - ❌ "Sonacoustic (Asona), Knauf, Fermacell, Kvadrat"
  - ✅ "Conservation-grade display cases, AR-coated glass"
  - ❌ "Glasbau Hahn"

## Language: No Self-Incriminating Statements

As contractor, do not include language that could be used against Samaya:
- ❌ `⚠ CRITICAL PATH` — implies schedule risk caused by Samaya
- ❌ "submission in progress" for items Samaya should have already submitted
- ❌ "pending approval" for items that are overdue
- ✅ Use neutral "TBC" with no risk labeling. Let the risk register speak for itself.

## Cover Page

- Do NOT include "SAMAYA INVESTMENT COMPANY / TECHNICAL OFFICE · BIM UNIT" text in the cover footer.
- Logo row at bottom: centered across full page width with `justify-content:space-between`. Label short (Client, PMC, Consultant, Designer, Contractor — not "Consultant (CG)").
- Document ref placeholder: omit until DC assigns a number. Only show "REV C02" (or current rev).

## Revision History

- All entries checked by **Project Manager**, not BIM Mgr or Tech Office.
- Dates: use actual document creation dates, not current date.
- C00 baseline date for Resource Management Plan: 2026-05-16.

## Staff Names

- Only list **KPR-approved names**. Do NOT invent or carry over names from prior revisions that aren't in the live KPR.
- Role-to-person mapping that must be correct:
  - Mohamed Mostafa = **Mechanical Engineer (HO)**, NOT BIM Modeler
  - BIM Modelers: Toka Hesham · Mohamed Matrawy · Alaa Hissi (no Mohamed Mostafa)
  - Do NOT list junior/unapproved engineer names
