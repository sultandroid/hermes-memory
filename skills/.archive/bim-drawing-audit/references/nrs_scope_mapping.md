## Naming Convention

- Register uses: `A2742-XXXX`
- Files for existing/demo/sections: `A2742-A-XXXX`
- Some files have OneDrive suffix: `A2742-1100 (1).pdf`
- Always search both variants when cross-referencing

## Key Contractual Clauses

- Review period: ≤72 hours per submission
- NRS stamp = design intent approval only (not construction means/methods certification)
- NRS to attend online meetings whenever required
- NRS to attend in-person KSA meetings if required
- NRS stamp does NOT relieve Contractor of: coordination, dimensions, quantities, fabrication methods, installation, temporary works, statutory compliance
